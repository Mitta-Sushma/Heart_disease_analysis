About This Project

This project leverages health datasets to study heart disease risk factors including age, BMI, diabetes, smoking, alcohol use, and more. Through interactive dashboards and stories, users can explore patterns, correlations, and trends in population health data.

The visualizations provide insights into risk factors, helping healthcare professionals, researchers, and students understand key determinants of heart disease and make informed decisions.
